# YIRAH — The Fear of the LORD (יִרְאַת יְהוָה)

A beautiful, self-contained interactive theological deep-dive studying the Hebrew and Greek words for the "Fear of God", the paradox of "Fear not" and "Fear him" (built on the same roots), the spectrum of biblical terms, and historical voices throughout the tradition.

## Features

- **Etymological Analysis**: Explore 9 distinct Hebrew and Greek words (such as *yir'ah*, *paḥad*, *ḥarad*, *phobos*, *eulabeia*, and *deos*) translated simply as "fear" in English.
- **Scriptural Threads**: Deep-dives into the themes of Covenant, Wisdom, Worship, and the transition of fear from terror to wonder.
- **Historical Perspectives**: Key insights from historical voices (such as Rudolf Otto, C.S. Lewis, John Bunyan, and Thomas Aquinas).
- **Premium Interactive Design**: Clean dark mode with glowing typography, smooth micro-animations, particles/embers effects, responsive layout, and scroll-driven entry reveals.
